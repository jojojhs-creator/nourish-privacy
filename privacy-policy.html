Privacy Policy — VitaIQ

Last updated: June 25, 2026

VitaIQ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the VitaIQ mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Meal and nutrition data: Meals you log, meal plans you create, and custom meals you add.
  • Health data: With your explicit permission, we access steps, heart rate, sleep data, active calories burned, and nutrition data via Android Health Connect.
  • Camera: Used for meal photo logging and barcode scanning.
  • Microphone: Used for voice input features and voice-guided logging.
  • Activity recognition: Used to track physical activity and provide relevant health insights.
  • Notifications: We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send you push notifications regarding your health goals and app updates.
  • In-app purchases: Subscription and purchase data managed through RevenueCat. Note that no financial or credit card data is stored by us; this is handled securely by the app store.
  • Barcode scanning: We use Google ML Kit for scanning food barcodes. This data is processed on-device.
  • Analytics: Basic app usage analytics via Firebase to help us improve the app experience.
  • App preferences: Your settings and preferences within the app.

Health Data & Google Health Connect

VitaIQ's use of information received from Health Connect will adhere to the Health Connect Permissions policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only access the health data necessary for providing health insights and logging features, and we do not share this data with third parties for any purpose other than providing the VitaIQ service.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Firebase: For push notifications and anonymous usage analytics.
  • RevenueCat: To manage in-app subscriptions and purchases.
  • Google Health Connect: To sync health and fitness data from other apps.
  • Google ML Kit: For on-device barcode scanning and image processing.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect financial information, payment card details, precise GPS location data, or your personal contacts. Any sensitive data not explicitly mentioned in the "Information We Collect" section is not accessed or stored by VitaIQ.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve the VitaIQ app, manage your account and subscription, sync your health data for better insights, and respond to your support requests.

Data Storage

Your meal logs, nutrition data, and synced health insights are stored locally on your device and securely backed up to our servers to ensure a seamless experience across devices. Account information is stored securely on our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not display advertisements in VitaIQ.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

VitaIQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
